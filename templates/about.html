Why ObserveQ?

Classroom behaviour is complex, and manual observation is often biased, inconsistent, or simply not possible in large classes. ObserveQ uses privacy-aware AI to help teachers understand how students engage – without adding to their workload.

  • Real-time attention and engagement analytics
  • Behaviour patterns across days, subjects, and teachers
  • Early alerts for disengaged or at-risk students
