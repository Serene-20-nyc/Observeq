AI-generated coaching tips

Simple, practical suggestions based on repeated patterns across your classes.

  • Use “first five minutes” to reset focus

    In classes after lunch or at the end of the day, ObserveQ sees stronger attention in sessions that begin with a short recap quiz or warm-up question.

  • Alternate theory and activity segments

    Attention dips most often during long, uninterrupted explanations. Breaking these into smaller chunks with questions, pair work or demonstrations improves engagement.

  • Invite quieter students in low-stakes ways

    Classes with more balanced participation often use quick, low-pressure prompts (“turn and talk”, “write and share”) instead of only open class discussions.
