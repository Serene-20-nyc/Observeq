Parent Dashboard

Monitor your child's classroom behaviour and engagement with clear, objective insights.

Monitor, understand, take action

ObserveQ turns class data into a parent-friendly view.

  • Class-wise and student-wise analytics

    See how your child behaves and engages across different subjects and classes.

  • Attention, participation, and engagement trends

    Track whether focus and participation are improving, stable, or need attention.

  • AI-generated insights and flagged sessions

    Review sessions where behaviour patterns stand out, so you can discuss them calmly at home.

  • Downloadable reports for meetings

    Export clean summaries you can bring to parent–teacher meetings to plan next steps together.
