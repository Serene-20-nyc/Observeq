How ObserveQ Works

From classroom video to actionable insights in minutes.

1

Upload Classroom Video

Teachers upload recorded classroom sessions through our secure dashboard. Supported formats include MP4, MOV, and AVI files up to 1GB.

2

AI-Powered Analysis

Our privacy-first AI analyzes engagement patterns, attention levels, and collaboration behaviors while automatically blurring faces for privacy.

3

Generate Insights

Receive comprehensive reports with engagement scores, behavior trends, and actionable recommendations for improving classroom outcomes.

4

Track Progress

Monitor student engagement over time, identify at-risk students early, and celebrate improvements with data-driven parent-teacher conferences.
